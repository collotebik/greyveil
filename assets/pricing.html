Common Questions

What people ask
before they engage.

How is pricing determined?
All engagements are custom-scoped. Final pricing depends on scope, complexity, and organisation size. We always discuss your needs before issuing a proposal.
How does the quoting process work?
You submit an enquiry, we schedule a free 30-minute discovery call, and within 48 hours you receive a detailed written proposal scoped to your requirements.
Do you offer payment plans?
Yes. For larger engagements we offer milestone-based billing. Monthly retainer services are billed at the start of each month via M-Pesa, bank transfer, or card.
Can I bundle multiple services?
Absolutely — and we encourage it. Clients who combine services (e.g. pentest + SOC monitoring, or audit + consulting) typically receive a bundled discount of 10–20%.
Do you work with startups and SMEs?
Yes. We work with organisations of all sizes — from early-stage startups to large enterprises. We'll always right-size our engagement to match your budget and maturity level.
How quickly can you start?
Most engagements can begin within 5–10 business days of contract signing. For urgent incident response or critical assessments, we can mobilise within 24–48 hours.
