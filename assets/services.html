Penetration Testing
Offensive Security Get a Quote →

Find your vulnerabilities before attackers do.

Penetration testing — or ethical hacking — is the most direct way to understand how exposed your systems really are. Our certified security engineers simulate real-world attack scenarios against your infrastructure, applications, and networks using the same tools, techniques, and methodologies that malicious actors use in the wild.

Unlike automated vulnerability scans, our penetration tests involve skilled human operators who think creatively, chain vulnerabilities together, and uncover logic flaws that tools simply cannot find. Every test is scoped to your specific environment and risk profile, and every finding is documented with a clear severity rating and a practical remediation path.

We follow industry-standard frameworks including OWASP, PTES (Penetration Testing Execution Standard), and NIST SP 800-115 to ensure comprehensive, consistent, and defensible results.

  • Network Infrastructure Testing
  • Web Application Testing
  • Mobile Application Testing
  • API Security Testing
  • Social Engineering Simulation
  • Wireless Network Testing
  • Cloud Environment Testing
  • Detailed Report & Remediation Plan
  • Executive Summary
  • Re-test After Remediation
Managed Security (SOC)
24/7 Monitoring Get a Quote →

Always-on protection, so your team doesn't have to be.

A Security Operations Center (SOC) is the nerve centre of any mature security programme — a team of analysts continuously monitoring your systems for threats, anomalies, and active intrusions around the clock. Building one in-house is expensive and time-consuming. Greyveil's managed SOC gives you all the capability without the overhead.

We deploy SIEM (Security Information and Event Management) technology across your environment to collect, correlate, and analyse log data in real time. Our analysts triage alerts, investigate incidents, and respond rapidly to contain threats before they escalate. You receive regular reporting, so you're always informed about your security posture.

Our SOC is staffed by experienced analysts trained in threat intelligence, incident response, and digital forensics — giving you enterprise-level protection at a fraction of the cost of an in-house team.

Security Audits & Compliance
Risk & Governance Get a Quote →

Know exactly where you stand — and what regulators expect.

A security audit is a structured, independent assessment of your organisation's information security policies, controls, and practices measured against a recognised standard or framework. Our audits give you an honest picture of your current security maturity and a clear roadmap to improvement.

We are well-versed in the major compliance frameworks relevant to Kenyan and East African businesses, including the Kenya Data Protection Act (2019), ISO/IEC 27001, the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS), and GDPR for organisations with European exposure. We don't just check boxes — we help you build genuine security capability that satisfies regulators and protects your business.

Our compliance engagements include gap analysis, policy development, risk register creation, and ongoing advisory support through your certification journey.

  • PCI DSS Gap Analysis

Security Awareness Training
People & Culture Get a Quote →

Your people are your first line of defence. Train them accordingly.

The vast majority of successful cyberattacks begin not with a technical exploit but with a human mistake — a clicked phishing link, a reused password, an unlocked screen in a public space. Technical controls alone cannot solve a human problem. Security awareness training equips your entire workforce to recognise, resist, and report threats.

Greyveil delivers engaging, practical security training designed for the Kenyan business context. Our programmes are built around real scenarios relevant to your industry and your employees' daily tasks — not generic, out-of-context slides from a foreign market. We make security relatable, memorable, and actionable.

Our training programmes range from one-off workshops to ongoing awareness campaigns with simulated phishing exercises, monthly security tips, and tracked completion dashboards. We also offer specialised training for developers (secure coding), IT staff (technical security operations), and executives (boardroom cyber risk).
