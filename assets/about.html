Who We Are

Built on Trust.
Driven by Purpose.

Greyveil Technologies was founded with a singular conviction — that African businesses deserve world-class cybersecurity expertise rooted in a deep understanding of the local landscape.

Our Story

Security expertise,
African context.

Greyveil Technologies was established in Nairobi, Kenya, with the mission of bringing enterprise-grade cybersecurity and software engineering to businesses across East Africa and beyond. We recognized a critical gap — global security tools and frameworks designed without the unique realities of African infrastructure, regulation, and threat actors in mind.

We bridge that gap. Our team combines internationally recognized credentials with hands-on experience in the Kenyan and East African technology ecosystem. From fintech firms to healthcare providers to government agencies, we protect the organizations that matter most.

Our approach is never one-size-fits-all. Every engagement starts with listening — understanding your business, your risk tolerance, and your goals — before we recommend a single solution.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every engagement, every decision, and every line of code we write.

Integrity First
We operate with complete transparency. Our clients trust us with their most sensitive systems, and we honour that trust with honesty, rigour, and accountability in everything we do.
Continuous Growth
The threat landscape never stops evolving, and neither do we. We invest heavily in keeping our team at the forefront of global cybersecurity research, tools, and certifications.
Africa-First Mindset
We are proud Kenyans building for Africa. Our solutions are designed with local infrastructure realities, regulatory environments, and business contexts at their core.
Client-Centric Delivery
Our success is measured by yours. We don't deliver reports and disappear — we stay engaged, explain findings in plain language, and ensure every recommendation is actionable.
Precision & Rigour
In cybersecurity, close enough is never good enough. We apply methodical, framework-driven processes to every assessment, audit, and deployment — with zero room for guesswork.
Community Impact
We actively contribute to building Kenya's cybersecurity talent pipeline through mentorship, training initiatives, and partnerships with local universities and tech communities.
